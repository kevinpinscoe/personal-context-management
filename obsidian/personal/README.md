# obsidian/personal/ — Habits, Rationality, Behavior, Personal Growth

Distilled knowledge about personal development, habit formation, cognitive
patterns, decision-making, and behavior change.

## What Belongs Here

- Habit systems and techniques worth adopting or testing
- Mental models for decision-making and rationality
- Behavior change frameworks or strategies
- Personal productivity and focus approaches
- Insights about ADHD, autism, neuropathy, or cognitive load management

## Content Notes

- Frame entries as actionable — what to try, not just what was learned.
- Avoid re-summarizing content without extracting a concrete takeaway or
  experiment to run.
- If a technique directly relates to ADHD or autism accommodation, tag it
  `accessibility` as a subcategory.
